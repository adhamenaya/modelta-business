## Folder Descriptions

### **business-plan/**
Core business planning documents including vision, market analysis, financial projections, and strategy.

### **product-strategy/**
Product-focused planning including roadmap, features, use cases, and competitive advantages.

### **marketing/**
Marketing strategy, brand development, content, social media, and advertising plans.

### **sales/**
Sales strategy, customer personas, acquisition plans, partnerships, and customer success materials.

### **documentation/**
User-facing documentation and guides for learning the platform.

### **training-curriculum/**
Educational programs, courses, workshops, and certification paths.

### **partnerships/**
Partnership strategies, tiers, and agreements with various organizations.

### **financial-planning/**
Detailed financial models, funding strategies, and projections.

### **operations/**
Company structure, hiring, compliance, security, and operational processes.

### **marketing-materials/**
Pitch decks, one-pagers, presentations, and visual content.

### **web-presence/**
Website content, social media strategy, and community management.

### **analytics-metrics/**
KPI dashboards, success metrics, and performance tracking.

### **launch-plan/**
Pre-launch, launch, and post-launch activities and checklists.

### **customer-communication/**
Email templates, press releases, and customer success stories.

### **design-assets/**
Brand guidelines, logos, color palettes, and UI components.

### **legal-compliance/**
Terms of service, privacy policies, contracts, and legal documents.

### **community/**
Community guidelines, open-source strategy, events, and engagement.

### **reports/**
Quarterly/annual reports, market research, and competitive analysis.

### **templates/**
Reusable templates for business documents and processes.

### **projects/**
Quarterly initiatives and project planning.

### **innovation-lab/**
Research into future features and emerging opportunities.

### **knowledge-base/**
Glossary, industry standards, best practices, and reference materials.

---

## File Naming Conventions

- Use **kebab-case** for all files: `marketing-strategy.md`
- Use **snake_case** for directories with multiple words: `customer-personas/`
- Use **UPPERCASE** for main files: `README.md`, `STRUCTURE.md`
- Prefix files with date when relevant: `2024-q1-roadmap.md`

---

## Key Considerations

### 1. **Scalability**
- Structure grows with business
- Easy to add new sections
- Maintains clarity at scale

### 2. **Navigation**
- Clear hierarchy
- Cross-references between sections
- Central index in README

### 3. **Collaboration**
- Clear ownership areas
- Version control friendly
- Template-based consistency

### 4. **Governance**
- Compliance and legal materials organized
- Security and privacy documented
- Audit trails maintained

### 5. **Customer-Facing**
- Marketing and sales materials easily accessible
- Documentation structure mirrors product
- Community engagement tracked
